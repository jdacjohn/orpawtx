TSTC West Texas Institutional Effectiveness & Information Research

Rubrics On-Line Reference

The faculty members of each of the programs offered at Texas State Technical College West Texas have collaborated with their Program Advisory Committees to establish the specific soft skills, also known as employability skills or essential skills, that will be emphasized and integrated into the program curriculum.

On these pages, the soft skills selected through that collaborative process are listed for each program and each soft skill contains links to allow you to browse the competencies that comprise the soft skills along with the associated progression-based ratings of those competencies.

All soft skills are introduced in the Professional Development course required of all students.
